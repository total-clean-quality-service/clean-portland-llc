About Us

At Clean Portland LLC, we are committed to reducing litter and improving the quality of life for Portland's residents through sustainable, community-driven solutions.

Our vision is a cleaner, greener Portland, where public spaces are safe, welcoming, and free of debris.

Our Solutions

  • Targeted Cleanup Programs: Organizing efforts to clean parks, highways, and residential areas.
  • Community Collaboration: Partnering with local groups and city departments.
  • Public Awareness Campaigns: Engaging the public to reduce litter.
  • Job Creation: Offering employment opportunities to support cleanup efforts.
